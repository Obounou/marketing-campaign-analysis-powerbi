# 📊 Marketing Campaign Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🎯 Objective

This project aims to analyze marketing campaign performance and identify high-value customer segments in order to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 📁 Data Sources

This project combines two different datasets:

- Marketing campaign dataset (customer responses, channels, conversion)
- Customer dataset (demographics, income, education, marital status)

---

## 🛠️ Data Preparation

Several preprocessing steps were performed:

- Data cleaning (handling missing values, standardization)
- Feature engineering:
  - Age groups segmentation
  - Conversion indicator (binary)
  - Revenue aggregation
- Merging multiple data sources into a unified dataset

---

## 🧱 Data Modeling

A star schema was implemented:

- Fact table: campaign interactions & conversions
- Dimension tables:
  - Customer profile
  - Education
  - Age group
  - Marketing channel

---

## 📊 Key KPIs

- Conversion Rate: 12%
- Total Clients: 45K
- Converted Clients: 5289
- Average Revenue: 52K

---

## 📈 Analysis & Insights

### 1. Campaign Performance
- Mobile channel generates the majority of conversions (~82%)
- Significant variation in conversion depending on job category

### 2. Customer Segmentation
- Higher education levels (PhD, Master) correlate with higher revenue
- Customers aged 60+ have the highest spending
- Marital status impacts revenue distribution

### 3. Time Analysis
- Conversion rate shows seasonal variation
- Certain months perform significantly better than others

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Prioritize mobile marketing campaigns
- Target high-value segments (PhD, senior customers)
- Optimize campaigns during high-performing months
